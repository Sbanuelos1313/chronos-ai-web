The Founder

Samantha Banuelos

The Origin

It started on a yoga mat.

Late October, after spending a week helping friends navigate the chaos of job searching — bouncing between subscription sites, paying for access, finding ghost listings, algorithms that never responded — I ended class with a simple question: how can I help?

The answer came before I left the studio. I ran home, wrote it down, and spent hours mapping the system that would become Hypercruit. It wasn't a business plan. It was a response to a lifetime of watching qualified people fall through cracks that shouldn't exist.

"If these students can see that their passion can be turned into a profession — that there is actually a way of obtaining a career path outside of just what they see on paper — it is exciting. It gives them hope."

The Mission

My brother — Choctaw and Chickasaw — found his path through Oklahoma technology centers and tribal programs that saw him before the workforce did. My daughter is on the waitlist for Francis Tuttle's broadcasting program, waiting for her own door to open. I know what it means when that door opens. And I know what it costs when it doesn't.

"There is no glass ceiling. The sky will never have a limit. As long as you believe in yourself, you can accomplish anything."
