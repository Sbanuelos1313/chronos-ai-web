Disability Intelligence Agency

D.I.A.

The benefits veterans earned. Finally accessible.

Live

D.I.A. — the Disability Intelligence Agency — is a plain-language VA benefits claims platform that guides veterans through one of the most complex bureaucratic systems in existence. No legal background required. No confusion about what forms to file or what language to use.

Veterans describe their conditions in their own words. D.I.A. maps those conditions to VA/ICD diagnostic codes, pulls relevant BVA case law, auto-fills the correct VA forms, and generates fully formatted nexus letters — producing a print-ready claims package in minutes instead of months.

Plain-Language Input

Describe your condition the way you actually talk. D.I.A. handles the translation to VA/ICD codes.

BVA Case Law Engine

Automatically surfaces Board of Veterans Appeals decisions relevant to your specific conditions.

VA Form Auto-Fill

Populates the correct VA forms with accurate diagnostic language — no blank-staring required.

Nexus Letter Generation

Generates formatted nexus letters connecting service history to current conditions.

Claims Tracking

Monitor your claim status and document history in one organized dashboard.

Print-Ready Package

Complete, submission-ready claims package generated end-to-end.
