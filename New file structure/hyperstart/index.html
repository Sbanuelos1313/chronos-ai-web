HyperStart is a gamified STEM learning platform for K-6 students that doesn't just teach science and math — it starts building the career identity that follows them all the way through to Hypercruit.

The longitudinal pipeline is the point. A student who discovers they love systems design at age 8 should have a thread that connects that discovery to their first job at 22. HyperStart is where that thread begins.
